How To Play

Select a stock from the dropdown list
Select a quantity
Proceed with trades to populate your portfolio
When happy with your portfolio, hit "Update Day Count"
The day count will increase and the prices will adjust
Keep an eye on your unrealised gain/loss figures
If you want to sell a position, select the checkbox in Portfolio Valuation, and hit "Sell Stock"
Your portfolio will adjust and cash proceeds added to your "Cash For Investment"
